## Tips for Effective Outreach

### Best Practices:

1. **Personalize each email**
   - Reference their specific work
   - Explain why you contacted them specifically

2. **Be clear about what you need**
   - Specific files
   - Specific questions
   - Clear timeline

3. **Acknowledge their expertise**
   - Show you've done your homework
   - Reference their publications

4. **Offer appropriate compensation**
   - Scholars: Honorarium, co-authorship
   - Communities: Benefits agreement, control

5. **Be respectful of time**
   - Keep emails concise
   - Provide clear deadlines
   - Follow up politely

6. **Document all communications**
   - Keep records of feedback
   - Track changes made
   - Maintain acknowledgment list
