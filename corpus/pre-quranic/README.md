# Pre-Quranic Sacred Texts Collection

This directory contains sacred and religious texts that predate the revelation of the Quran (610-632 CE).

## Collection Overview

| Tradition | Text(s) | Language | Date Range |
|-----------|---------|----------|------------|
| **Judaism** | Tanakh (Torah, Nevi'im, Ketuvim) | Hebrew | ~1200-200 BCE |
| **Judaism** | Dead Sea Scrolls | Hebrew, Aramaic | ~300 BCE-100 CE |
| **Christianity** | New Testament (Gospels, Epistles) | Koine Greek | ~50-100 CE |
| **Christianity** | Gospel of Thomas | Coptic/Greek | ~100-150 CE |
| **Hinduism** | Vedas (Rig, Sama, Yajur, Atharva) | Sanskrit | ~1500-500 BCE |
| **Hinduism** | Upanishads | Sanskrit | ~800-200 BCE |
| **Zoroastrianism** | Avesta (Gathas, Yasna) | Avestan | ~1000-500 BCE |
| **Buddhism** | Pali Canon (Tripitaka) | Pali | ~300 BCE-100 CE |
| **Taoism** | Tao Te Ching | Classical Chinese | ~400-300 BCE |
| **Confucianism** | I Ching, Analects | Classical Chinese | ~1000-400 BCE |
| **Ancient Egypt** | Book of the Dead, Pyramid Texts | Hieroglyphic | ~2400-50 BCE |
| **Mesopotamia** | Epic of Gilgamesh, Enuma Elish | Akkadian | ~2100-1200 BCE |

**Pre-Quranic Timeline:**

- ~2100 BCE: Epic of Gilgamesh (Mesopotamia)
- ~1500 BCE: Rigveda (India)
- ~1300 BCE: Torah composition begins (Israel)
- ~1000 BCE: Avesta Gathas (Persia)
- ~800 BCE: Upanishads begin (India)
- ~500 BCE: Tao Te Ching (China)
- ~300 BCE: Pali Canon compiled (Sri Lanka)
- ~200 BCE: Dead Sea Scrolls begin (Judea)
- ~50 CE: New Testament writings begin
