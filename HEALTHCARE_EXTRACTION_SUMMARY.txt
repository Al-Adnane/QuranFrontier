================================================================================

Quranic Source:
- Q87:1-3: "Day and night cycles, creation with perfect proportioning (taqdir)"
- Q73:6, Q25:47, Q36:37-38: Night for rest, day for activity
- Q80:17-18: Harmony between natural rhythms and biological function

Mathematical Formalization:
✓ Circadian Oscillator: ~24.2 hour free-running period
  - dφ/dt = 2π/24.2 hours (phase evolution equation)
  - Phase shift function: ΔPhase = A × sin(2π(t_stimulus - phase_ref) / 24.2)

✓ Melatonin Profile: M(t) = M_base + M_amp × sin(2π(t - t_peak)/24 + φ_shift)
  - M_base ≈ 5-10 pg/mL (baseline)
  - M_amp ≈ 20-50 pg/mL (individual variation)
  - Peak at 2-3 AM (variable by chronotype)
  - Suppression by light (E_half ≈ 100 lux)

✓ Cortisol Pattern: Cor(t) = Cor_base + Cor_amp × sin(2π(t - t_peak)/24)
  - Peak 30 min to 1 hour after wake (CAR: 40-60% rise)
  - Steady decline through day
  - Minimized at sleep onset
  - Elevation in chronic stress: Cor_elevated = Cor(t) × (1 + stress_factor)

✓ Body Temperature: T(t) = T_base + T_amp × sin(2π(t - t_peak)/24)
  - Amplitude ~0.5°C per day
  - Peak at 5-6 PM, low at 4-5 AM
  - Optimal sleep at lower temp (18-19°C room)
  - Metabolism increases 10-13% per °C

✓ Homeostatic Sleep Pressure: H(t) accumulates during wake, dissipates during sleep
  - Accumulation: ΔH = k_accumulate × Δt / (1 + activity_level)
  - Dissipation: ΔH = H_sleep(t) × k_dissipate × sleep_quality
  - NREM3 (deep): 1.0 restoration effectiveness
  - REM: 0.8 effectiveness (memory consolidation)
  - NREM1: 0.3 effectiveness (insufficient)

✓ Phase Adjustment Rates:
  - East travel (phase advance): ~40% per day (~2-3 hours max/day)
  - West travel (phase delay): ~50% per day (~4-6 hours max/day)
  - Asymmetry reflects evolutionary pressures (sunset vs. sunrise)

Alignment Score: 1 - (Phase_Shift / 12)
- Perfect alignment at scheduled sleep time = 1.0
- 12+ hour shift = 0.0 (maximum misalignment)
- Each hour of shift = -0.083 score reduction

Sleep Consolidation Formula:
- 0.4×duration_score + 0.3×latency_score + 0.3×fragmentation_score

Sleep Regularity Formula:
- exp(-bedtime_variance/1.5) × exp(-waketime_variance/1.5) × weekend_penalty
